For long quotes, use the 'blockquote' tag and the cite="website" attribute. For example:

Sea slug is a common name for some marine invertebrates with varying levels of resemblance to terrestrial slugs. Most creatures known as sea slugs are gastropods, i.e. they are sea snails (marine gastropod mollusks) that, over evolutionary time, have either entirely lost their shells or have seemingly lost their shells due to having a significantly reduced or internal shell.[1] The name "sea slug" is often applied to nudibranchs and a paraphyletic set of other marine gastropods without apparent shells.

For short quotes, use the 'q' tag. Browsers will insert quotation marks. For examlpe: this is a quote!

For abbreviations use the 'abbr' tag. Example: The WHO was founded in 1948.

For contact information, use the 'address' tag. It defines the contact info for the author of a document/article. For example:

Written by John Doe.
Visit us at:
Example.com
Box 564, Disneyland
USA

To cite the title of a creative work, use the 'cite' tag. For example:

The Scream by Edvard Munch. Painted in 1893.
